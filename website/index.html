About Us

We are a modern startup that combines sustainable food supply and people well-being. As metropolitan workspaces become more and more cramped, we want to increase the well-being of inhabitants and employees, as well as work towards a sustainable future. We bring the nature indoors, by growing edible plants right next to your desk. Imagine no longer having to import fruits and greens from overseas to your table. Instead, many of the imported greens can be locally grown, where you play every day, all year.
