SCANDINAVIAN DESIGN MEETS SUSTAINABILITY

"Most of my food has travelled more in its lifetime than I have in mine”



By 2050, it's projected that 68% of the world's population will live in urban areas. Soon, there will be fewer people farming the land (and also the land itself) than there are people to feed.

Traditional agriculture systems are less and less reliable due to its high freshwater consumption and exposure to climate change.



In Sweden the fresh food supply chain is unsustainable. Imported food accounts for 70% of the climate impact in Sweden.

By the time the food reaches our table lots of energy and waste has been created. In addition, the food loses a significant level of its nutritional value.



We are increasingly aware of the consequences of our modern relationship with food. We like to have access to non-native fruits and vegetables all-year-round. This habit will put more strain into the food supply chain as urbanization increases. Though well-being is high in the Nordics, we spend a lot of time indoors which affects our health

Our Mission

Our mission is to offer designed tech solutions that revolutionizes the relationship between urban vertical farming, sustainable food supply and people's well-being. We focus on service excellence to provide solutions that meets the specific needs of our customers.

How Hollbium helps

At Hollbium, we design experiences, not products

When we bring solutions into this world, we are responsible for ensuring it meets a genuine human need while minimizing the impact it has on the planet. We provide a concept that combines scandinavian design, sustainable food tech and people's well-being. Through Hollbium’s solutions you can make a positive impact on securing the city’s food supply in a sustainable way.

Hollbium combines hydroponics and LED technology with tailored solutions and services to enable everyone in successfully growing greens all-year-round.

About Us

We are a modern startup that combines sustainable food supply and people well-being. As metropolitan workspaces become more and more cramped, we want to increase the well-being of inhabitants and employees, as well as work towards a sustainable future. We bring the nature indoors, by growing edible plants in your workspace or home. Imagine no longer having to import fruits and greens from overseas to your table. Instead, many of the imported greens can be locally grown, all seasons of the year.
